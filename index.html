Brief Biography

Neuroscientist turned software engineer

My programming interests developed out of necessity in academic research to create standardized data analysis procedures. As time has gone on, the pull towards computer science has become unignorable, and I seek to continue optimizing work flows, building full stack products, and analyzing data throghout industry. With 4 years of scripting and professional experience in academia and 3 years working in full stack development, I made the move to merge my database and full stack engineering skills into a quantitative analytics role; I bring a unique combination of knowledge and practical application in learning, teaching, collaborating, and self-starting projects from the database to the user, with foresight on project impact and application across all technical needs.
